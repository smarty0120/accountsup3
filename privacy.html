Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Accountsup.com handles information submitted through this website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you submit through contact and consultation forms, including your name, email address, company, service interest, jurisdiction, preferred time, and message details.

How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to enquiries, schedule consultations, understand service fit, and maintain a record of business communications.

How We Protect Information

We limit access to submitted information to people who need it to respond to your request. Do not submit passwords, tax IDs, payroll files, bank details, or confidential records through public website forms.

Form submissions may be processed through configured form handling and email tools. Those services may process data under their own privacy and security terms.

Your Choices

You may request correction or deletion of information submitted through this site by emailing info@accountsup.com.

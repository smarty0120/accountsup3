Operating Process

How We Keep Accounting Work Moving

Instead of publishing unsupported performance claims, this page explains the operating rhythm we use to keep books, payroll records, and compliance tasks organized.

Workflow

A Clear Monthly Operating Cadence

Each engagement is scoped around the systems you use, the deadlines you face, and the level of review your team needs.

Step 01

Collect

Gather access requirements, statements, payroll exports, prior filings, and open questions in one request list.

Step 02

Reconcile

Review transaction records, bank feeds, AR/AP items, payroll entries, and tax-related balances.

Step 03

Review

Flag missing information, unusual balances, filing deadlines, and items that need management approval.

Step 04

Report

Deliver close summaries, payroll support notes, filing status updates, and next-month priorities.

What We Measure

  • Open information requests by owner and due date
  • Bank and card accounts reconciled for the period
  • Payroll records received and matched to ledger entries
  • Sales tax, GST/HST, payroll, and year-end filing deadlines
  • Management questions requiring approval or escalation

What We Do Not Claim Without Evidence

We do not publish client counts, accuracy percentages, security certifications, or savings claims unless they can be verified and explained. If a formal case study is relevant to your business, we can discuss available examples during a consultation.
